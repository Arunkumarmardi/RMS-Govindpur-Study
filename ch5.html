Overview: MS Paint provides simple drawing tools: Pencil, Brush, Shapes, Fill, Text, Eraser. Use Resize & Crop to adjust images. Save as PNG/JPEG.

Tool Details

  • Pencil: Draw thin freehand lines.
  • Brush: Different stroke styles and sizes.
  • Fill: Fill closed shapes with color.
  • Text: Add and edit text boxes in images.
