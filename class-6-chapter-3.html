From Fibre to Fabric

English: The process of making fabric from fibre includes several steps:

  1. Collection: Getting cotton, jute, wool, or silk fibres.
  2. Spinning: Fibres are twisted to make yarn.
  3. Weaving/Knitting: Yarn is made into fabric using looms or needles.
