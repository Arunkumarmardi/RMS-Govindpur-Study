Water | जल

Water cycle, uses & conservation — brief notes and examples.

English — Brief

Water is essential for life. It exists in three forms: solid (ice), liquid (water) and gas (vapour). The water cycle includes evaporation, condensation and precipitation.

Examples: Rain forms when vapor condenses into clouds; lakes store fresh water for animals and plants.
